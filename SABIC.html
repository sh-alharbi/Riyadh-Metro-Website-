Welcome to SABIC station!

The Riyadh Metro is a major public transportation project in Saudi Arabia’s capital, launched in April 2014 to reduce traffic congestion and support urban growth. Partial operations began in 2021, with full completion planned soon. The system spans six lines over 176 kilometers with 85 stations, including the iconic SABIC Station, known for its innovative design and strategic location. Featuring modern, driverless trains and advanced signaling, the metro’s design blends modern and traditional Saudi architecture while prioritizing accessibility and sustainability. It aims to enhance mobility, support smart city initiatives, and boost local businesses and job creation.
